What is Oregon Alternative Senior Care?

OASC is a single family residence that functions as an adult foster home (AFH) for aging and adults with physical disabilities (ADP). We offer around the clock care that is tailored for each resident according to their needs. Meal prep including dietary preferences and restrictions, medication administration, stand-by assistance. If you can think of it, we have people able to do it.

A single family residence.

What is the home like?

From big rooms to small rooms, shared rooms to private rooms as well as multiple bathrooms we have everything to make your loved ones feel at home. The backyard has a covered patio to keep the sun at bay and the rain off your skin. There is also a path that wraps around the side of the house for walking exercise. For those more inclined to stay indoors we have activities as well as television, on-demand and multiple streaming options available.

Whom should I contact?

Call or email any time.
